pancakes

heres some lovely pancakes to give a try.

ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/3 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

directions

  1. mix flour, baking powder, salt and sugar into large bowl, then whisk to combine the egg, vanilla extract, milk and melted butter together.mix them untill its combined
  2. in a large frying pan, over medium heat, melt 1 tbsp of butter. pour /4 of batter into pan
  3. when little bubbles appear on top of the batter, flip it. keep checking on it until both sides are lightly golden
  4. stack it on a bowl and enjoy. if you enjoy syrup, pour it over

enjoy!
