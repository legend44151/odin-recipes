Vanilla milkshake

im someone that loves vanilla milkshakes.

i thought, hey why not share it with one of you guys who'd be interested in making one

ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

directions

  1. grab a blender
  2. blend the ice cream, milk and vanilla extract together until smooth
  3. dump it into a cup

enjoy!... sb add some whip cream on top if you have some laying around
