white rice

simple tasty white rice

ingredients

  • 1 cup of jasmin rice or brand you prefer
  • 1/2 salt
  • 1 tbsp of cut scallion

directions

  1. In a large pot saute scallions in a litle bit of oil medium heat
  2. after a few minutes, add 1 1/2 cups of water and salt then wait for boil
  3. taste for salt, add more if needed, else add the rice
  4. once the rice absorb almost all the water, reduce heat to low to allow cooking to finish
  5. after 10+ minutes check to see if done, get a bowl with some sauce and...

enjoy!
